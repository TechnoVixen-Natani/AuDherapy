Common Inquiries

🦴 What is "Forensic Tracking"?
It is a high-precision data method used to identify logical glitches in daily regulation. Instead of vague moods, we track specific metrics like medication timing and sensory triggers.
🔒 How secure is my data?
The app utilizes a Master PIN system and Session Authorization. All data is stored locally on your device's browser memory (LocalStorage), meaning it never leaves your hardware.

Operational Procedures

How to update your Master PIN:

1 Click the ⚙️ gear icon on the dashboard.

2 Enter your current PIN for forensic verification.

3 Enter your new 4-digit code to receive the 🐾🐾 SUCCESS 🐾🐾 confirmation.
